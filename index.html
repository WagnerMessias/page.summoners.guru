Your voice app for League of Legends

Summoners Guru is a voice application that provides information about the game league of legends, it will be available in Google Assistant can be used on any device with screens or speakers that have the virtual assistant of Google,

And the cool thing is that you do not need to install to use it just say "Ok Google, Talk to Summoners Guru" and you will have access to the information about the champions and the free weekly rotation, soon new features will be released.

Google Assistant

Awesome features

Free champion rotation

See the free champions to play in the week.

Get notifications

Receive notifications when the new free champion rotation has been launched.

Champion Information

Speak or type the name of the champion you want and see his basic information.

Skins

After choosing a champion say you want to see the skins.

Champion's Abilities

See the skills available for a champion hot keys Q, W, E, R and Passive

Detail of Abilities

See details of the champions' abilities including passive.
